Hi, I'm Jesse.

I wanted to give a little background on why I love what Brave Care is building and what has brought me to want to work on something for kids and where my interest in the health industry came from.
Where to start

Happy and Healthy Kids.

Without diving into my childhood, I remember actively thinking at a lot of times “Don’t forget what this feels like when you’re older”.

There was always a sense of wanting to help kids, when I was older, to not go through whatever I went through. Like I think we all try to learn from our lives and help others with what we learn.

I was always the youngest though, even in my small extended family, so I wasn’t around kids much at all, but that all changed a few years ago...

Meet my nephew, Henry.

In the fall of 2015 my sister had her first kid, Henry.

I commuted from home my last semester in college and my mom took care of Henry for forty hours a week and I helped out whenever I was around.

He became my sidekick, or maybe yet I became his sidekick.
humpty dumpty

A bump on the head...

The first time I was watching him by myself, he fell and bonked his head on something on the way down. In retrospect, it probably was a fall that a seasoned parent wouldn’t even flinch at or recall, but I can still feel the fear that rushed through me like nothing I had experienced before as I called my mom in a panic.
